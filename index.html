ABOUT

In case you’re wondering who is behind all of these…

You can call me Tonie. ✌🏻😘 I am a Filipina based in Canada. I like coffee shops, rainy days, and sticky notes. ☕️☁️ I am a registered nurse in-the-making. 💉

My love for reading started with a Disney Fairytale book. 🧚🏻‍♀️ Since I was eight, I have been a fan of short stories, with “The Little Match Girl” as my first read. 📖 Through the years, I grew up close to reading as well as writing. ✍🏻 I started collecting books in 2014, with 65 books and counting. 📚✨ My favourite picks are YA Fiction, Romance, Fantasy, and Mystery. 🙌🏻👌🏻 I have tons of favourite authors, with Cassandra Clare, Marie Lu, Jasmine Warga, Holly Bourne, and Jonah Mae Pacala to top them all.

To this day, I am still reading and discovering new stories. 😍 On my free time, I am either at the bookstore searching for a new release, or on a coffee shop rereading another favourite. 🤭⭐️ In between, I like to create poems and write my novels. 🪐⚡️ Aside from that, I am also into vlogging, photography, art, fashion, and travelling. 📸✈️

I guess that sums it all up. Although I created this website as a project, I still wanted to work on something that I am interested and passionate about. 🪐💫Thus, I choose a book blog. In here, you will see my latest reads 🔎, favourite authors 💛, book reviews🎖, and so much more! 📚✨

Feel free to discover my digital library! 👩‍💻💛
